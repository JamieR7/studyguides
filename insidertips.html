‚úì Model Answer

‚úì Model Answer
The LIP describes the intensity of exercise where lactate production exceeds the rate of removal. Training near this point improves aerobic endurance.

B.2 Forces & Motion

SL / HL ~10-15 Marks
üí° Tip: Newton's Laws are the "Money Topic" for calculations.
Paper 2 (Calculations)
Explain how an athlete can increase their acceleration using Newton's Second Law (F=ma). [3 marks]
‚úì Model Answer
Since Acceleration = Force / Mass:
1. The athlete can increase the Force applied to the ground (ground reaction force).
2. The athlete can reduce their Mass (though less practical in competition).
3. Therefore, applying greater force results in greater acceleration.
