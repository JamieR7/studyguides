What to Expect: Sample Questions

Paper 1A | Knowledge MCQ
Topic A.1: Which component of the homeostatic control mechanism detects a deviation from the set point?
  • A. Effector
  • B. Control Centre
  • C. Sensor (Receptor)
  • D. Negative Feedback Loop
‚úì Correct Answer: C
Explanation: The Sensor detects changes. The Control Centre decides the response. The Effector acts.
Paper 1B | Data Analysis 3 Marks
Scenario (Topic A.2): A study compared sleep habits. Elite athletes: 49.5% "No problems". Sub-Elite: 25.0% "No problems".

(a) Calculate the difference in percentage. [1]
(b) Distinguish between the two groups. [2]
‚úì Model Answer
(a) 49.5% - 25.0% = 24.5%

(b) Elite athletes had significantly fewer sleep problems. Nearly double the percentage of elites reported "no problems" compared to sub-elites.
