Paper 1A | MCQ
"Which class of lever provides a range of motion advantage but a mechanical disadvantage?"

A. 1st Class
B. 2nd Class
C. 3rd Class
Correct: C (3rd Class)
Why? The effort is close to the fulcrum (like a bicep curl). You have to pull hard (force disadvantage) but the hand moves a long way (speed/ROM advantage).

Paper 2 | Linking Question
"Explain how high-intensity training (Theme A) might influence intrinsic motivation (Theme C)."
High Mark Answer:
Success in HIIT leads to physiological adaptation (e.g. lower resting HR). Seeing this data improves Self-Efficacy (Bandura). This feeling of Competence (Self-Determination Theory) increases intrinsic motivation.
